ABOUT ME

Hey! My name is Kashika Dhanjal and I am a rising senior at Thomas Jefferson High School for Science and Technology. I am interested in both biology and tech, and how the intersections of these two fields can help solve many of todays problems. Currently, I serve as the Director of HackDefy and Sponsorship Director for Codefy and I am also a Publicity lead at Project YCRO. During my time in high school, I have served as the co-leader for an independent research project looking at the effect of diet in a strain of flies afflicted with a neuronal disease and have served as a staff writer and photographer for the TJ Yearbook. My academic pursuits regularly extend outside the classroom, as I have taught myself both Python and HTML/CSS. Outside of my academic pursuits, I am also interested in photography, fashion, and skincare, all of which will be detailed in my blog. In my free time, I like to listen to the Weeknd, watch Netflix, or read a good book.
